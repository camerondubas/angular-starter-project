While we recognize that minors may wish to use the Services for their fundraising projects, for their protection, we prohibit their use unless they have the consent and supervision of their parent or legal guardian.

If you are under the age of 13, you may only use the Services if your parent or guardian does so on your behalf. If you are under the age of 18 (or the ‘age of majority’ as such term is defined in your home jurisdiction, whichever is greater) then you may only use the Services with the permission and supervision of your parent or legal guardian.

If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has used the Services without consent, the parent or legal guardian should contact us at support@changeheroes.com so that we can immediately remove the child’s information from our Sites and databases.
